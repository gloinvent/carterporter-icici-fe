CARGO MANDATORY BOOKING INFORMATION

Complete address of sender and receiver will be updated by CarterX personnel 24 hours prior to date of pick up or alternatively the same can be updated by calling customercare.

Pick up of order will be done. Before 7pm and slots will be auto allocated. Delivery will be made before 11.55 pm on the date of delivery indicated.

Cargo Sender and Receiver must certify status of his/her health through the Aarogya Setu App or any other Govt regulated application/website.

CarterX will follow all govt protocols during pick-up and delivery

Orders from containment zones will not be collected as per government regulations.

Green/Safe is mandatory for pick up/delivery of order. If the area is a containment zone then the order will be processed for the next available day for pick up or delivery.

Security Declaration and Packing List must be duly filled and signed and stuck on the package at the time of pick up.

All items needs to be packed in ONE package and if there are any leakages during pickup then the package will be refused on the same grounds on inspection.

Food, beverages or any open items will not be carried.

Multiple bookings can be picked from the same pick up and delivered in the same location.

Cargo acceptance is independent of CarterX acceptance of the package. The package will be returned to same address of pickup if the package is refused for any reason. INR 399 plus GST will be deducted from cargo amount or billed for delivery of package to the address of pick up. The balance of the order amount if any will be refunded automatically.

Pick-up and delivery pincode will need to be within the city urban perimeter. Any area outside the urban perimeter the order will be automatically refunded if pickup is not initiated. If delivery pincode is outside the perimeter of urban city limits then INR 12/Kms will be charged for extra kms incurred in making the delivery in advance payment via link.

Maximum dimension of each package cannot exceed 319 linear cms ( 81cm (H) x 119cm (W) x 119 cm (D) ).

Maximum weight can be up to 40kgs per piece. Excess weight will be charged for any order beyond prescribed weight purchased in the order selected.

Booking pick up address can be modified upto 24 hours prior via email to customercare@carterporter.in

Customer Care Bookings are confirmed post payment.

No pick up during curfew times imposed by the government.

All bags of each order will be picked at the doorstep to minimise exposure. No additional Packing will be provided.

Passenger/household personnel should continue to wear mask while interacting with CarterX personnel at pickup and delivery location.
