Booking Information

Departure: Bags will be delivered till the check-in counters

Arrival: Bags will be collected at Baggage Belts

Doorstep and Curbside pick up and delivery both are available.

Curbside includes to or from airport parking or cab drop off points and pick up lanes of the airport terminal only.

Departing passenger needs to meet CarterX personnel before entering the terminal

Arriving Customer needs to meet CarterX personal before exiting the terminal

Passenger must have completed his/her Web Check-in prior to the pick up slot time

Passenger ID proof is mandatory at the time of pick up and will be validated on delivery before entering the terminal

Security Declaration must be duly filled and signed for doorstep pick up.

The Passenger must arrive at the airport at least 120 minutes prior to departure for both doorstep and curbisde pickup.

Multiple bookings can be made under a single PNR

Charges for excess number of bags will be collected by the airline as per the airline directive.

We advice that excess weight be purchased on airline website for shorter wait times at counters.

Maximum dimension of each bag cannot exceed 319 linear cms ( 81cm (H) x 119cm (W) x 119 cm (D) ).

Maximum weight can be up to 32/kgs per piece as per CarterX carriage and is not indicative of airline weight limitations or excess weight.

Excess weight should be purchased prior or during the time of pickup from the airline website at doorstep for smoother pick up and delivery.

Bags cannot be added/subtracted once booking is confirmed.

Booking pick up address can be modified upto 24 hours prior

Doorstep Departure bookings are accepted upto 24 hours prior to departure.

Curbside departure can be booked and availed up to 3 hours prior to meeting carterX at airport.

Customer Care Bookings are confirmed post payment

All electronic items/oversized will be charged as Fragile with INR 399 per Kg as order modification piece.

All electronics should be in their original packing only

Booking pick up address will be updated post the booking and can be modified upto 24 hours prior for local orders only

Keep bags packed before porter arrives.

CarterX personnel will call prior to allocate Suitable Pick up Slot Time and complete the address from the given pin code

All orders will be delivered only on receipt of full payment of order and order modification
