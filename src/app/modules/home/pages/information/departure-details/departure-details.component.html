Booking Information

Departure: Bags will be delivered till the check-in counters
Arrival: Bags will be collected at Baggage Belts

Departing passenger needs to meet CarterX personnel before entering the terminal
Passenger ID proof is mandatory at the time of pick up and will be validated on delivery
before entering the terminal
Security Declaration must be duly filled and signed
The Passenger must arrive at the airport at least 120 minutes prior to departure
Multiple bookings can be made under a single PNR
Maximum dimension of each bag cannot exceed 319 linear cms ( 81cm (H) x 119cm (W) x 119 cm (D) ).Maximum weight can be up to 32/kgs per piece.
Bags cannot be added/subtracted once booking is confirmed.
All electronic items/oversized will be charged as Fragile with INR 399 per Kg as order modification piece.
All electronics should be in their original packing only
Booking pick up address will be updated post the booking and can be modified upto 24 hours priorÂ for local orders only
Keep bags packed before porter arrives.
No pick up during curfew times imposed by the government All bags of each order will be picked at the doorstep to minimise exposure.
Passenger/household personnel should continue to wear mask while interacting with
CarterX personnel at pickup location and the airport
CarterX personnel will call prior to allocate Suitable Pick up Slot Time and complete the
address from the given pin code
All orders will be delivered only on receipt of full payment of order and order modification
