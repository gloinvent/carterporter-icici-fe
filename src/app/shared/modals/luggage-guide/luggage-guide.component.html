We accept each luggage within the dimension.

The maximum permissible weight limit for each luggage is 40Kgs (Unless specified otherwise)

The maximum permissible height for luggage is 32 inches / 110cms

We cannot take bags over this weight or height for the enchanced service quality and health and safety of our drivers

Cabin Luggage (24 x 18 x 10 inches / 61 x 46 x 26 cms)

A Cabbin Luggage is a carry on bag that would fit in an airline overhead compartment

Checked In (34 x 23 x 14 inches / 87 x 59 x 36 cms)

A Checked In luggage is a bag that would be checked into airline for the duration of travel

Coats and Jackets Not Applicable

Any loose piece of clothing.Each piece will be counted as a seperate luggage item falling in to cabin luggage category.

Food & Beverage (24 x 18 x 10 inches / 61 x 46 x 26 cms)

a. Non Perishable items. Only packed and sealed items including restaurant categorized food & beverages for immediate consumption.
b. All food and beverages should be sealed to be leak proof.
c. Duty free items will be accepted only if the items are sealed as packed inside the airport.

Fragile (13 x 17 x 5 inches / 33 x 44 x 13 cms)

Any item of value or any item that need special care in transportation.
a. Portable electronics like laptops,cameras are included in this category.
b. Duty free items will be accepted only if the terms are sealed as packed inside the airport.
c. Any luggage beyond 5Kgs will be only accepted post physical inspection and discretion.
